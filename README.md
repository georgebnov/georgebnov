### Hey, I'm George 👋

I'm a 20 year old developer in Toronto. I build things that I want to understand to the bone, mostly around AI agents, ML models, and data systems. Currently focused on turning real estate data into something actually useful for agents and buyers.

- 🇨🇦🇦🇲🇰🇿 Based in Toronto. Armenian and Kazakh roots.
- 🧠 Obsessing over focus, flow states, and how to use AI to amplify thinking instead of replacing it.
- 🤖 Building AI agents that score leads, orchestrate tasks, and manage data pipelines.
- 🎸 Play fingerstyle guitar when I'm not on a screen.
- ♟️ Recently picked up chess. Still terrible.
- 🌍 Speak English, Russian, French. Understand Spanish. Failing at Chinese haha.
- 💬 Ask me about **AI agents, data architecture, productivity, or why most devs are getting dumber with AI**.

Some of the things I'm building or have been a maintainer for:

- 🏠 sift - AI lead scoring for real estate agents. Figures out which buyers are serious.
- 🏥 [locvm](https://github.com/locvm) - Locum doctor booking platform for clinics. ([Locvm](https://www.locvm.ca))
- 📦 avqsklad - ERP, database, and QuickBooks all in one. Tracks everything in one spot. ([Avateq](https://www.avateq.com))
- 📡 logreader - Signal analysis and coverage prediction tool for UHF and VHF waves. ([Avateq](https://www.avateq.com/logreader/))
- 🎙️ avatalks - AI content generator that writes in your voice using real data. ([AVAtalks](https://avatalkapp.com))
- 📚 biblioteka - Home library lending system. Tracks who borrowed what.
- 🧪 [LLM-Training-And-Learning](https://github.com/georgebnov/LLM-Training-And-Learning) - Training models and breaking models on my own hardware.

I'm always building something. If any of this is interesting, reach out.
